# Methodology Notes

## Analytical Approach
This project follows a standard business analytics workflow:
1. Define business problem
2. Identify business questions
3. Prepare and validate data
4. Build analytical features
5. Perform exploratory data analysis
6. Visualize findings
7. Convert findings into business recommendations

## Why Production Alone Is Not Enough
Production can increase because:
- harvested area increased,
- yield improved,
- both increased,
- one factor compensated for the other.

Therefore, production must be interpreted together with harvested area and yield.

## Volatility Score
Volatility is calculated as the standard deviation of year-over-year production growth by crop. A higher score means more instability in annual production.

## Data Limitation
The current dataset is synthetic. It is only for portfolio demonstration and training. Official analysis should use verified national or international data.
